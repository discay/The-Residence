PRIME LOCATION

Kandy,
One of Sri Lanka's
most desirable cities.

Richmond Hill Residence is perfectly positioned in the prestigious Hanthana hills — one of Kandy’s most exclusive and scenic residential neighbourhoods. Surrounded by lush greenery and offering sweeping panoramic views, the property delivers an exceptional lifestyle just minutes from the city’s most iconic landmarks.

Enjoy effortless access to the world-renowned Peradeniya Botanical Gardens, the sacred Temple of the Tooth, and Kandy’s vibrant city centre. The University of Peradeniya, top-rated restaurants, and the beautiful Digana Golf Course are all within easy reach. For those looking to explore further, the scenic hills of Ella and Nuwara Eliya are just a comfortable drive away — making The Residence an ideal base to experience both the cultural richness and natural beauty of Sri Lanka’s hill country..
